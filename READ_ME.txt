This is a project that is done because of a friend asked for it.

As most of my projects , i worked in "DB First" in this one as well.

Thats why i placed the Backup file of the Database inside the folder.

To make the project work , first you must attach this backup file to your Database.

Then you should change the connection string inside the app.config

And then you are good to go.

------------------------------------Features------------------------------------------

You can add ,delete and change the incomes and outcomes of your company.

You can see the results of your income outcome and revenue as daily or however you choose on the Reports(Rapor) tab.

You can see the results of your income outcome and revenue by monthly on the charts(Grafik).

You can add and delete employees and keep track of the money that you gave them monthly.

This program records the unauthorized login attemps if you have a webcam. So on the Notifications(Bildirimler) tab

you can see them and delete them.

Program also sends an e-mail to the owner if login try is failed 3 times on a row. You can edit the mail settings from database.

Also you can backup your database via backup button on the Settings(Ayarlar) tab. 

If you change the mail settings you should also change the SendEmail method inside the Helpers class.
